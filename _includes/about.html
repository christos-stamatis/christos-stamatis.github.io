About Me


I am a Chemical Engineer, fascinated with air quality, wildland fire science, and computational science.

I am currently pursuing my Ph.D (5th year) in Chemical and Environgmental Engineering from the University of California, Riverside (UCR). Currently I am working with Dr. Kelley Barsanti. Our group focuses on studying air qualiy by combining both field work and air quality modelling

My current research focuses on the development of algorithms for pattern recognition analysis of wildland fire smoke and faster processing of two dimensional gas chroamtography with time-of-flight mass spectrometry (GCxGC-TOFMS) data using Python.
